Minecraft Meets Diablo II + Path of Exile!

Craft to Exile is a modpack designed around exploration and progression. Mods in the modpack aim to enhance the players' experience with Mine and Slash. This mod draws inspiration from popular action RPGs such as Diablo II and Path of Exile.

Craft to Exile has a focus on progression with more dimensions becoming available as levels increase.

Included Mods:
- The Bumblezone, Endergetic Expansion, Good Night's Sleep, The Midnight
- Custom Quests (with full progression log)
- Traverse: Legacy, Valhelsia Structures, Dungeon Crawl
- MineColonies, Simple Farming, Immersive Engineering
- Mods integrated into Mine and Slash progression including LycanitesMobs and Spice of Life: Carrot Edition!
- And many more!
